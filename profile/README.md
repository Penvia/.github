# <img title="Logo" alt="Sprouting" src="/graphics/misc2-128.png"> Penvia

### The high performance bridge between your Android stylus and your Desktop.

[![Discord](https://img.shields.io/discord/1076480545640435742?color=7289da&label=Join%20the%20Alpha&logo=discord&logoColor=white)](https://discord.gg/uxG2kqGzUX) [![Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/Status-In%20Development-orange)](#) [![Platform](https://img.shields.io/badge/%20Cross%20Platform-blue)](#)

**Penvia** is a low-latency input communication bridge designed to make your Android device into a peripheral for your PC. Whether you're an artist, student or a teacher, Penvia ensures your stylus feels like it's connected directly to your desktop.

### Why Penvia?

* **Very low latency:** Optimized for speed, You are only limited by your network.
* **No annoying ads:** No banner ads, no popups, no tracking. Just your workflow.
* **Crossplatform:** Native support for **Windows, Linux, and Android**. (Web support for Apple users is on my mind)
* **Lightweight & Headless:** Minimal RAM footprint. You can run it as a background task or a headless server.
* **Full stylus support:** Complete support for Pen Pressure, Tilt (X/Y), side buttons, and toggles.
* **Not just for pens:** Full finger/touch support, swipe all you want.
* **Multi-screen support:** Works fine on multiple monitors.
* **Flexible connections:** Connect instantly over **Wi-Fi**, or use **USB** for maximum speed and stability.
* **Multiple connections:** Supports multiple simultaneous connections. Draw with your friends or use it alone.
* **Very Stable:** Build for extended uptime, perfectly suitable as a background task.
* **Reliable versioning:** Backwards compatibility is a priority, minor version updates don't break the setup.
